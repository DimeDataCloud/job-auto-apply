Dell Technologies — Inside Sales Account Executive

Location: Nashville, TN (Hybrid)
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role:
Dell Technologies is seeking a driven Inside Sales Account Executive to join our Sales Academy program. In this role, you will be responsible for driving revenue growth through outbound prospecting, qualifying inbound leads, and managing a portfolio of SMB and mid-market accounts. You will own the full sales cycle from initial outreach through close, working closely with field sales engineers and solution architects to deliver tailored technology solutions.

Key Responsibilities:
- Conduct high-volume outbound prospecting via phone, email, and social selling to build a robust pipeline of new business opportunities
- Qualify inbound leads and marketing-generated opportunities, routing them through the appropriate sales motion
- Manage a dedicated book of accounts, driving renewals, upsells, and cross-sells across Dell's full portfolio (servers, storage, networking, client devices, cloud, and software)
- Run discovery calls to understand customer business problems, technical requirements, and budget constraints
- Deliver compelling product demonstrations and ROI conversations tailored to SMB and mid-market buyers
- Navigate live objections, negotiate pricing, and close deals using a structured sales methodology
- Maintain accurate pipeline and activity data in Salesforce CRM, meeting daily and weekly KPI targets
- Collaborate with internal teams including solution architects, field sales, pricing specialists, and fulfillment
- Participate in ongoing sales training, coaching sessions, and certification programs through Dell Sales Academy

Required Qualifications:
- Demonstrated track record in outbound sales, cold calling, or field sales roles
- Strong communication skills with the ability to build rapport quickly and handle objections in real time
- Comfort with technology products and the ability to have credible technical conversations with IT buyers
- Discipline and resilience to sustain high daily activity volume in a metric-driven environment
- Experience using CRM systems (Salesforce preferred) and managing a personal pipeline
- Self-starter mentality with strong organizational and territory management skills
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience; sales training certifications a plus

Preferred Qualifications:
- Prior experience selling technology, SaaS, or telecommunications solutions
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure, server/storage/networking concepts
- Experience building or working with AI-powered software products
- Bilingual capabilities a plus

What We Offer:
- Structured sales development path with Dell Sales Academy
- Base salary plus uncapped commission
- Full benefits package including health, dental, vision, 401(k)
- Ongoing product and sales methodology training
